🧠 What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a long-term metabolic disorder that affects how your body converts food into energy. Normally, your body breaks down food into glucose (sugar) and releases it into the bloodstream.

In diabetes, either the body does not produce enough insulin or cannot use it effectively. This leads to high blood sugar levels which can damage organs.

Type 2 diabetes is the most common and is strongly linked with lifestyle factors.

⚙️ How Diabetes Occurs

  • Insulin deficiency
  • Insulin resistance
  • Hormonal imbalance
  • Improper glucose metabolism

⚠️ Symptoms

  • Frequent urination
  • Excessive thirst
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow healing wounds

📌 Causes

  • Genetics
  • Obesity
  • Lack of exercise
  • Stress
